Todo

This web Application is a digital tool designed to help you manage tasks and stay organized. Some key features include Task Creation, where you can add tasks to your list, specifying details like due dates, priorities, and descriptions. Task Organization, tasks can be grouped into categories or projects. For example, you might have separate lists for work, personal, or shopping tasks. Priority Levels, you can assign priority levels (e.g., high, medium, low) to functions to focus on what’s most important. Due Dates and Reminders, setting due dates for tasks and receiving reminders to complete them on time. Checklists and Subtasks, breaking down larger tasks into smaller subtasks, or creating checklists within a task. Cross-Platform Syncing, many apps sync across devices (web, mobile, desktop) so you can access your tasks anywhere finally, Completion Tracking, marking tasks as done when completed, and they’ll move to a separate completed section.
